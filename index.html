Our Customer Onboarding Process

Cross Industry Methodlogy integerated with our customised Implementation First Approach

  • Business Understanding

    Understanding primary objective from a business perspective. Coming up with the project plan for achieving business goals. Laying out the Definition of project success metric in sync with business criteria

  • Data Understanding

    Mapping data sources in sync with business processes.Preparing data readiness and data quality reports. Exploratory data analysis to validating the business and problem solution hypotheis.

  • Data Preparation

    Process include Rationale for inclusion/exclusion, data wrangling, generating feature pool of derived and native attributes. Tranforming the data sources into the receipe input of the model.

  • Modelling

    Selecting the modelling technique in sync with business problem. Generating the test and evaluation design . Building the model with tuned parameter settings. Preparing the summarised business view of model results leading to business feedback loop into the modelling exercise

  • Evaluation

    Summarise assessment results in terms of business success criteria, including a final statement regarding whether the project already meets the initial business objectives. Identifying the potential further actions, along with the reasons for and against each option.

  • Deployment

    Deployment of final successfully reviewed model,service to the public/private cloud or on-premise . Summarise the monitoring and maintenance strategy, including the necessary steps and how to perform them.
